FAQ

Common Questions

Is there any fee to register?
No. Registration, profile creation, and listing are completely free. Only 15% commission on completed treatments.
How and when is the commission paid?
When a patient completes treatment, 15% of the treatment fee is invoiced. Payment is due within 30 days.
What if the patient comes but doesn't get treated?
You pay nothing. Commission only applies to completed treatments. All risk is on Clinfly.
Which treatment areas are supported?
We currently provide patient referrals for hair transplant and face aesthetics.
Can multiple clinics be on the platform?
Yes. Clinfly is a multi-clinic marketplace. Patients compare clinics and choose the best fit for their needs.
How do we communicate with patients?
Through in-app messaging (text, photos, documents, voice). Direct WhatsApp communication is also available.
Do we manage our own profile?
We handle initial setup. Updates to pricing, photos, and info are done instantly upon your request.
Where do patients come from?
We focus exclusively on patient referrals from the United States.
