Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

Is there any fee to register?
No. Platform registration, profile creation, and listing are completely free. Only a 15% commission is charged on completed treatments.
How and when is the commission paid?
When a patient completes treatment, 15% of the treatment fee is invoiced as commission. Payment is due within 30 days of invoicing.
What happens if the patient comes but doesn't get treated?
You pay nothing. Commission only applies to completed treatments. All risk is on Clinfly.
Which treatment areas are supported?
We provide patient referrals for hair transplant and face aesthetics.
Can multiple clinics be on the platform?
Yes. Clinfly is a multi-clinic platform. Patients compare clinics and choose the best fit for their needs. This model helps you reach a wider patient base.
How do we communicate with patients?
Through the in-app messaging system, you can send text, photos, documents, and voice messages. Direct communication via WhatsApp is also available.
Do we manage our own profile information?
We handle the initial profile setup. After that, we provide support whenever you want to update your information. Package pricing, photo updates, etc. are done instantly upon your request.
Where do patients come from?
We currently focus on patient referrals from the US market.

Information We Need to Get Started

To showcase your clinic at its best, we need the following information.

🏥

Clinic Information

Clinic name, address, year established, accreditations (JCI, ISO, Ministry of Health), operating hours, supported languages, WhatsApp, and email.

Doctor Information

Full name, specialty, years of experience, total procedures performed, education, certifications, professional photo, and languages spoken.

Treatment Packages

Package name, detailed inclusions list, net USD price (all-inclusive), hotel info, transfer, PRP/medication/follow-up included, cancellation policy.

Exterior, treatment room, patient room, waiting area photos (minimum 5, high resolution).

🔄

Patient-approved before/after photos (minimum 10). This is the most viewed content on the platform.

Guarantee Policy
